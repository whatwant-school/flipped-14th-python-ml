
## bias vs. variance

![bias_variance](img/bias_variance.png)

### bias
실제값에서 얼마나 멀리 있는가

### variance
예측된 값들이 서로간에 얼마나 멀리 떨어져 있는가



## Underfitting vs. Overfitting

### Underfitting
- too bias

### Overfitting
- high variabces, less bias





## How to overcome overfitting?

- Regularization
- Cross Validation
- Add more data as much as possible
